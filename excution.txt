When you input 'Tatweel' after the first character, it will automatically do it for the rest of the word
In preferences you can adjust rules of 'Tatweel', like how many and between what characters
You can highlight the word and input 'Tatweel' so that it is applied on the word between each two characters
Exceptions for 'Tatweel' should be handled

السلام عليكم (0 Tatweel)
